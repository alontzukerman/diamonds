[02] Diamond
Stone

The stone shape refers to the geometric form of the cut and directly affects the stone’s appearance, light reflection, and visual proportions. Different shapes emphasize different qualities, such as brilliance, symmetry, or surface area. The choice of shape is primarily an aesthetic decision and does not indicate the stone’s quality or origin.

Source

A natural diamond is considered more valuable than Lab because it's rare, limited, and tied to a long tradition of status and symbolism.

Carat

Carat refers to the weight of the diamond. Larger diamonds are rarer and more valuable.
